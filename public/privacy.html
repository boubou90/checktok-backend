Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 4, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use CheckTok, we collect the following information from TikTok:

  • Your TikTok username
  • Your profile picture
  • Your TikTok user ID
  • Your saved TikTok videos list

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the CheckTok service. We personalize your experience and help you organize your saved videos. Your data is used to:

  • Authenticate your identity
  • Display your saved TikTok videos
  • Organize and categorize your content
  • Provide AI-powered insights about your saved videos

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We use AES-256-GCM encryption for sensitive data such as access tokens. Your data is stored securely and is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Supabase, a trusted database provider. We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data at any time. You can revoke CheckTok's access to your TikTok account at any time through your TikTok account settings. Upon request, we will delete all your data from our systems.

6. Third-Party Services

CheckTok uses the following third-party services:

  • TikTok API - for accessing your saved videos
  • Supabase - for secure data storage
  • OpenAI - for AI-powered content analysis (optional feature)

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and local storage to maintain your session and improve user experience. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us.
